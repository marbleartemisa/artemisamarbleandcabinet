From a small family workshop in Hialeah to a high-precision manufacturing operation powered by European CNC technology, Artemisa Marble & Cabinet has redefined custom luxury production in South Florida.

Origins

The Beginning

Founded in 1975, Artemisa Marble & Cabinet was born from a vision rooted in craftsmanship, family values, and dedication to quality. Named after Artemisa, Cuba — hometown of its founder — the company began as a modest cultured marble vanity top workshop in Hialeah, Florida.

What started with manual cutting tools and artisanal techniques evolved into one of South Florida’s most respected custom fabrication operations.

The Evolution

Chapter I
The Beginning

Founded in 1975 in Hialeah, Artemisa Marble & Cabinet began as a small family workshop dedicated to cultured marble craftsmanship and precision detail. Under Damian Molina, the company unified carpentry and stonework into a single production philosophy, transforming traditional artisanal methods into a structured manufacturing system.

Chapter II
Evolution

From manual fabrication to CAD engineering and CNC automation, Artemisa evolved into a hybrid manufacturing ecosystem where precision, scalability, and craftsmanship coexist in perfect balance.

Chapter III
Technology & Craft

Breton CNC, SCM Pratix C12 and Olimpic K560 define a new era of precision manufacturing where technology enhances human craftsmanship instead of replacing it.

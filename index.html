What area are you based in? +
Our main client base is located in the state of Florida, on both coasts. The majority of our clients are located in Miami, Napo, Cape Coral, Fort Myers, and Tampa. However, distance is not an obstacle to offering our services.
How long does a typical project take? +
Project timelines vary depending on the scope of the contracted results; however, we consider an average of 4 to 6 weeks to be a reasonable timeframe. You should keep in mind that a kitchen or bathroom renovation can take time not only due to the physical dimensions of the area to be remodeled, but also due to the complexity of the project, the elements involved, and the technologies the client wishes to incorporate. A kitchen that includes translucent stone walls, columns, hardware to conceal outlets, or more sophisticated door opening mechanisms will obviously require more time, and in exceptional cases, availability from the supplier or the import process is required.The success of the project also depends on taking the required time; rushing through stages is not a good option.
Do you offer free quotes? +
Yes! We offer free quotes, and we can even evaluate different alternatives without obligation. After the initial contact, we assign a project manager who will analyze your needs and expectations for the project with you. We will provide you with a detailed quote. We can discuss, modify, and find the best solution to achieve a balance between your needs and costs.
Do you provide a guarantee for your work? +
Absolutely! We guarantee the quality of our work and the materials we use; we always choose the best manufacturing and installation technologies. We always advise clients on the appropriate course of action and inform them of the disclaimers that apply in each case. In any case, your peace of mind is guaranteed by the excellence we demand in every project.
Can I stay in my home while the work is being done? +
Our work isn't particularly invasive. Everything is manufactured in our workshops. Many elements are transported already assembled, and only the installation takes place at your home. However, there may be minor disturbances due to noise, dust, and other elements inherent in the installation and handling of the manufactured stones.
How do I get started with a project? +
Simply contact us through this website, email info@artemisamarble.com, or call +1 (305) 884-1223. We will assign you a Sales Executive to discuss all your expectations and needs, prepare the budget and work schedule, outline your responsibilities as a counterpart, and guide you through all stages of the project.
